# 22.2 Cod or Not? (Marine Ecosystem)

## Model Metadata
- **Chapter**: 22 – Advanced SD Cases
- **Type**: Advanced Case
- **Domain**: Ecology / Fisheries
- **Simulation Period**: 1985–2050
- **Time Step**: 0.25 year
- **Integration Method**: Euler

## Description
Trophic cascade model of the cod population off Nova Scotia. After overfishing collapsed large predatory fish (cod, haddock, pollock, hake) around 1990, prey fish increased, zooplankton decreased, phytoplankton increased. Since 1999, prey fish declining (own food shortage), allowing cod larvae to survive again.

## Key Stocks
- large_predatory_fish (cod, haddock, etc.)
- prey_fish (herring, capelin, sandeel)
- zooplankton
- phytoplankton
- cod_larvae

## Key Dynamics
- Fishing ban in 1993
- Predator-prey reversal
- Trophic cascade through 4 levels
- Damped oscillation recovery pattern (since 2005)
