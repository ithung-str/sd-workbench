# 14.7 Tolerance, Hate and Aggression

## Model Metadata
- **Chapter**: 14 – Simple SD Cases
- **Type**: Simple Case
- **Domain**: Social Science / Conflict
- **Simulation Period**: 0–50 years
- **Time Step**: 0.25 year
- **Integration Method**: Euler

## Description
Social dynamics model of how tolerance, hate, and aggression interact in a population with in-group/out-group dynamics.

## Stocks
| Stock | Initial Value | Unit |
|-------|--------------|------|
| tolerant_population | 90% | fraction |
| hateful_population | 8% | fraction |
| aggressive_population | 2% | fraction |

## Key Dynamics
- Transition between tolerant → hateful → aggressive states
- Triggering events increase hate
- Social pressure and policing reduce aggression
- Media amplification effects

## Sensitivity Analysis
- Vary triggering event frequency and magnitude
- Vary social cohesion factors
- Test policy interventions: education, policing, media regulation
